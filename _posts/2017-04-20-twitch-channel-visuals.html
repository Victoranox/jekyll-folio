Beautiful Machine Visuals

This illustration commission was for a cyberpunk themed banner and buttons for a twitch homepage. The conceptual development for the banner and icons was a lot of fun and in the end resulted in the creation of 5 unique icons, custom circuit board textures, a vivid colour scheme, and a scalable banner image to create an online visual identity.

By staying true to the theme of cyberpunk, the visuals are consistent with each other and work together to create a professional profile for the user. The banner image combines the users interest in machinery with his love of vivid colours and futuristic technology.

I am personally very happy with the final result: a profile true to the interests and personality of the streamer.
