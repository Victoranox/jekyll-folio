Starling Concept Stills

This is a conceptual study of a scene from an online short story. The aim was to create two still frames expressing the different perspective of each character. I was successful in taking a written concept and creating visuals that not only depicted the scene, but the underlying themes of mutual discomfort and alienation. There is harmony between the two characters, portrayed by the vivid coloured lighting that washes out their skin tones- yet the posture and distinct camera angles maintain tension between the two. The image was received positively amongst the community and I hope to maybe continue illustrating scenes as the story progresses in my spare time.
